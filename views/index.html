A little bit about me.

A budding software developer living and working in Indore, India. I come from a small town in the Indore. I resided here until I moved on, at 23, to study at SGSITS Indore, Indore. It has taken me a while to get to this place (having previously chosen to study Architecture) but I am more than happy to say I have finally found a home in Software Engineering. I have a passion for cutting-edge technology, design, business and mathematics, so an industry that is a compilation of all four is proving to be a perfect fit for me.

I have many interests, many strengths and skills that allow me to be highly adaptive to various roles. I’m a strong programmer and designer, but equally as strong a business and marketing mind. I have a sound eye for promising business opportunities and I’m not afraid to give my honest opinion on those ideas that I believe to be a dead end. I regularly have several exciting projects on the go at once and hate not having anything to get stuck into.

I am driven by a desire to gain valuable experience on challenging projects and hope to one day have my own business (or several :p). I offer a meticulous attention to detail with a keen eye for design to help mold innovative ideas. I'm constantly striving to contribute positively to any working situation and thrive on the prospect of new challenges.
